GIVEAWAY+ PRIVACY POLICY
Last Updated: 16.02.2025

1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how the Giveaway+ ("Bot") application collects, uses, and protects user data. By using the Bot, you are deemed to have accepted this policy.

2. Collected Data
We collect and process the following data to provide our service:
- **User ID and Username:** To track participation in giveaways and determine winners.
- **Server ID (Guild ID):** To record which server the giveaways are held in.
- **Message IDs:** To manage and update giveaway messages.
- **Role Data:** To organize role-restricted giveaways, etc.

3. Use of Data
Collected data is used solely to perform the bot's core functions (creating giveaways, ending them, selecting winners, keeping statistics).

4. Data Sharing
User data is not shared, sold, or rented to third parties, except for legal obligations.

5. Data Retention and Deletion
- Giveaway data is stored in the database for history (history) and statistics (stats) features.
- If you wish for your data to be deleted, you can contact the developer via the bot's support server.

6. Changes
This policy may be updated from time to time. When significant changes are made, an announcement will be made via the bot or on the support server.

7. Contact
For questions regarding the privacy policy, you can reach the developer via Discord.
